# Refund Policy

All purchases are eligible for a full refund within 30 days of purchase.

## Conditions

- The product must be unused and in original packaging
- Digital products can be refunded if less than 10% of content has been accessed
- Refund requests must be submitted through our support portal

## Processing Time

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after approval. The refund will appear on your original payment method.

## Exceptions

- Sale items are final sale and cannot be refunded
- Custom or personalized products are non-refundable
- Subscriptions can be cancelled but the current billing period is non-refundable
